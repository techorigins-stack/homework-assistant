Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Plain-language summary. We keep data collection to a minimum. This website is a simple informational site — we don't run our own advertising trackers, and we only use the contact details you choose to send us (for example, by email) to reply to you. This document is a general template and is not legal advice; please consult a qualified professional for a policy tailored to your specific situation.

1. Overview

This Privacy Policy explains how Homework Assistant ("we", "us") handles information in connection with our website and related services (the "Service"). We are committed to handling any information responsibly and to collecting only what we need.

2. Information we collect

  • Information you give us. If you contact us — for example by emailing support@techorigins.io — we receive your email address and whatever you choose to include in your message.
  • Basic technical data. Like most websites, our hosting provider may automatically log standard technical information (such as IP address, browser type, and pages requested) for security and reliability. We do not use this to personally identify you.
  • Third-party fonts. This site loads web fonts from Google Fonts, which may receive your IP address as part of serving those files. See Google's privacy policy for details.

3. What we do not do

  • We do not sell or rent your personal information.
  • We do not run our own advertising or cross-site tracking cookies on this website.
  • We do not knowingly collect more information than necessary to operate the Service and respond to you.

4. How we use information

We use the limited information we receive to respond to your enquiries, provide and improve the Service, maintain security, and comply with our legal obligations. We process information only for these purposes.

5. Cookies

This website does not set its own tracking cookies. Your browser may store standard items required for basic functionality. You can control or delete cookies through your browser settings at any time.

6. How we share information

We do not share your personal information except: (a) with service providers who help us operate the Service (such as our hosting and email providers), under appropriate confidentiality obligations; (b) where required by law or to protect our legal rights; or (c) with your consent.

7. Data retention

We keep information only for as long as needed for the purposes described in this policy, or as required by law, after which it is deleted or anonymized.

8. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect information against loss, misuse, and unauthorized access. However, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

9. Your rights

Depending on where you live, you may have rights to access, correct, or delete information we hold about you, or to object to certain processing. To make a request, contact us at support@techorigins.io and we will respond in line with applicable law.

10. Children's privacy

The Service is intended for students and general audiences. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children in a manner that conflicts with applicable law. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will take appropriate steps.

11. International users

The Service may be accessed from around the world. By using the Service, you understand that any information you send us may be processed in the country where we or our service providers operate.

12. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date above reflects the most recent revision. Material changes will be posted on this page.

13. Contact

Questions about this policy or your information? Email us at support@techorigins.io.
